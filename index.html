New Zealand Adventure

Explore the stunning natural beauty, with mountains, glaciers, beaches, and rainforests. It is also home to a unique culture that blends Maori and European influences.

Aoraki/Mount Cook National Park

Named after New Zealand's highest peak, is a majestic wonderland of soaring mountains, glaciers carving through valleys, and star-studded skies. Immerse yourself in the grandeur of the Southern Alps, hike alongside icy giants, and experience the tranquility of a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where nature reigns supreme.

Milky way

One of the best countries to observe milky way. With relatively light pollution, it is easy to see millions of stars at night.

Lake Tekapo

Nestled amidst the Southern Alps, Lake Tekapo shimmers like a turquoise jewel framed by snow-capped mountains. Renowned for its stargazing opportunities due to minimal light pollution, this UNESCO World Heritage Site offers breathtaking views by day and transforms into a celestial spectacle by night. Witness vibrant lupins bloom along the shore in spring, adding a touch of color to this picture-perfect paradise.
